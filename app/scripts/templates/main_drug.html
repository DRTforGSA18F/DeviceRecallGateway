FDA Drug Recall Classifications

FDA Drug Recall Classifications (classification – OpenFDA data field name) link for see FDA 101: Recall Classifications

Class I: Dangerous or defective drug products that predictably could cause serious health problems or death.

Class II: Drug Products that might cause a temporary health problem, or pose only a slight threat of a serious nature.

Class III: Drug products that are unlikely to cause any adverse health reaction, but that violate FDA labeling or manufacturing laws.
